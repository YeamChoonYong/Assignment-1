What is Bandit Records?

Bandit Records is one of the most recent music labels to join the EDM Music Scene. The label was originally founded in the year 2021 in the small island of Singapore. Ever since then, the label has been mainly making it's mark known in the Indie EDM Music Scene across Asia. The label's main style of music is inspired by the sounds of fast paced energetic electronic music that is mainly found in Arcade Rhythm Games such as Maimai DX, Taiko no Tatsujin and Sound Voltex. The main signature of a Bandit Records Song are Japanese Inspired Melodies along with fast transitions and high BPM.

Our Vision

To spread the culture of EDM to a wider audience and create a family out of artists across one label.

How Did It Begin?

Bandit Records was first founded in 2021 by EDM Producer Star Bandit. Star Bandit is a Singaporean Artist who started gaining popularity after many of his tracks were featured in the Sound Voltex Franchise. Being extremely passionate about Rhythm Game Music, he decided to start his own label that is mainly directed at promoting Rhythm Game Music in an attempt to not only open the world up to it, but to also meet other producers who feel the same way about rhythm game music.

How has Bandit Records has changed the world?

"Before I started releasing my music on Bandit Records, I was an extremely small producer who was mainly releasing on sites such as Newgrounds. I am a huge rhythm game fan and it was one of the reasons that I started producing music. When I first heard about Bandit Records, I was intrigued by it's concept. When I joined, I met a community who had the same amount of joy that I have when it came to rhythm games. My experience started to become more positive when I started releasing my music there, with a lot of love from the community about how my music was what they had been looking for. Bandit Records is definitely a rare sight to behold and I have no regrets joining." - Hydra (Bandit Records Producer)
